Rice pudding

Ingredients

  • Caster sugar2 tbsp
  • Vanilla extract1 tbsp
  • Thickened cream300 ml
  • Coles Strawberry Jam80 g
  • Coarsely chopped pistachios1 tbsp
  • White rice450g
Rice pudding is a dish made from rice mixed with water or milk and other ingredients such as cinnamon, vanilla, and raisins.

How to cook:

1. Heat the rice following packet directions. Transfer to a heatproof bowl. Sprinkle with the sugar. Add vanilla and 3/4 cup (185ml) of the cream and stir to combine.
2. Divide rice mixture evenly among four 1-cup (250ml) microwave-safe bowls. Cook in the microwave on high for 2 mins or until heated through.
3. Top the rice mixture with a dollop of jam and use a spoon to gently marble. Drizzle with the remaining cream and sprinkle with pistachio.
